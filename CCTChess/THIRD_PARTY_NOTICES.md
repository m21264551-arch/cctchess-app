## Lichess Open Database

`CCTChess/Resources/lichess_fens.lzfse` contains bundled chess positions derived from Lichess database exports.

Source: https://database.lichess.org/

Lichess states that its database exports are released under the Creative Commons CC0 license. The app bundles a transformed FEN-only, LZFSE-compressed subset for offline tactical training.
