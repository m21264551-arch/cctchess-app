# CCT Chess Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

CCT Chess is designed as an offline chess training app. It does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

## Data Collection

CCT Chess does not collect personal data from you.

The app does not use:

- Account login
- Analytics SDKs
- Advertising SDKs
- Tracking SDKs
- Third-party network services

## Data Stored On Your Device

CCT Chess stores the following information locally on your device:

- Puzzle attempt history
- CCT rating and games played
- Board theme
- Piece style
- Training preferences, such as hidden move counts

This information is used only to operate the app and personalize your training experience. It is not sent to the developer or to third parties.

## Network Access

CCT Chess works offline. The bundled puzzle database is included with the app.

The app includes an acknowledgement link to database.lichess.org in the Acknowledgements screen. Opening that link leaves the app and is handled by your browser or system settings.

## Device Backups

Local app data may be included in your iCloud or encrypted device backups depending on your device backup settings.

## Deleting Data

You can remove locally stored CCT Chess data by deleting the app from your device.

## Children

CCT Chess does not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated when the app changes. The updated version will be posted at this URL.

## Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the support URL listed on the CCT Chess App Store page.
