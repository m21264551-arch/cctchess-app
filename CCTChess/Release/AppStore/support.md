# CCT Chess Support

Need help with CCT Chess?

## Common Questions

### Does CCT Chess require an account?

No. CCT Chess works without login or account creation.

### Does CCT Chess work offline?

Yes. The puzzle database is bundled with the app.

### How do I reset my progress?

Delete the app from your device to remove local progress, rating, and preferences.

### What does CCT mean?

CCT stands for Checks, Captures, and Threats. The app trains you to scan for tactical candidates in that order.

### Why did a category move on when I tapped "Record misses"?

"I'm stuck" opens a confirmation. "Record misses" intentionally moves to the next category and counts any remaining moves in the current category as missed.
